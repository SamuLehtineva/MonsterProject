#Start
Press E to interact with the world and to move forward in dialogue.


Use WASD to move around.


You can also press R to pet your creature.


The pink bar in the bottom left corner indicates how much your creature likes you.


The blue bar in the top right corner shows your reputation in the village.


Beneath the reputation bar is your wallet. It shows how much money you have to spend.


When a round icon appears in the top left corner it means the quest log has been updated and you can open it by pressing esc.
